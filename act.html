Some of the most interesting and fun locations in Toronto

Here is a list of just some of the many interesting and intriguing locations you can find in and near Toronto.

The CN Tower

The CN Tower is one of the most unique buildings in Toronto and helps give it a very distinct and recongizable skyline. It is one of the tallest towers in the world and the largest free-standing structure in the Western Hemishpere, coming in at 1,815 feet tall. Inside the tower you can look outside and enjoy the view or eat inside the tower's resturante. But who cares about the inside of the tower when you can do the EdgeWalk which lets you walk along side the outside of the tower, over 100 stories in the air!

Niagra Falls

Just about an hours drive away from Toronto lies one of the worlds most famous waterfalls, Niagra Falls. Besides watching the famous waterfall there are other fun activites you can partake in at Niagra Falls such as zip lining, a museum, a maze and an indoor water park! Niagra Falls has something for everyone, so you're not gonna want to miss it.

Toronto Zoo

The Toronto Zoo is a great location to vist as it will let you see a wide varity of plants and animals from across the globe. The zoo houses over 5,000 animals that belong to over 500 species, making it the largest zoo in Canada. The zoo also is a big helper in conservation so any money you spend there will be supporting a good cause. A small warning, the Zoo has been occasionally closing down or temporailiy disabling certain exhibits due to Covid concerns. Though even if you don't get the chance to vist the zoo, you won't have to miss out on all the fun because their website has live cameras of some of their animal exhibits

MZTV: Museum of Television & Archive

This museum sets itself apart from other television museums by being focused on the technology of televions, whereas most similar museums focus on televison programs. The museum has over 10,000 objects to view, with a large focus on televison history fro the 1920's to 1970's. For that range of years the museum has the largest collection of objects. Anyone who finds televisons interesting is surely gonna want to vist this museum.
