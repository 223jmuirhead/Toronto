History

Learn about the rich history of the city Toronto

Before becoming a city, the land that Toronto resides on was inhabited by the Seneca Tribes. When colonizer from Europe came many of the Natives died because of new diseases the Europeans brought with them like Small Pox.

The city was founded in 1793 by American colonist who still supported the British. At this point the City was called York, not Toronto yet. It didn't get the name Toronto until the year 1834. Many loyalist went to the city after America gained it's independence.

Image of Toronto in the early 1900's

In 1812, during the War of 1812 the City was attacked by U.S. troops and it got pillaged. Nowadays the city has become the financal and commerical centre of Canada. The Great Lakes allows for easy trade with American Ports. The city is also very diverse because there was a lot of immigration into the city during 1950's to the 1970's.
